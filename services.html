Can I update my website on my own post-launch?

Yes, your website is built with scalability, editibility, and performance in mind. Modular CMS is the way to go if you want to customize, add, or remove any section on your website. This gives you the freedom to rely on no developer and even no designer when you know that you can edit while keeping design and flow consistency all across your web pages.
